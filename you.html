Settimeout in ES6

The variable i is declared in the for loop and is incremented from 0 till i is less than 20. So when we are printing i, the values of i from 0 to 19 are printed

In the setTimeout function, the value of i which is 20 is printed for 20 times at an interval of 1 sec.
